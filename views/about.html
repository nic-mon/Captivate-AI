How to Use
  • 1. After installation, click Add-ons -> Captivate AI -> Start Here
  • 2. By this, a dialog window will open and ask you some questions about the presentation you are about to give.
  • 3. After this dialog, a second dialog will appear, asking you to brainstorm intial thoughts about the theme of your presentation.
  • 3a. You have the option of skipping this brainstorming session if you don't want to fill it out. Our tool uses this brainstorm to generate new content for you.
  • 4. After you either skip or fill it in, the sidebar will appear. It has 3 sections: Ideas, Home, and Quick Fixes.
  • 4a. Ideas has suggestions for new content, such as typeface changes, new images, and new quotes.
  • 4b. Fixes are quick fixes such as fixing alignment of objects or suggesting less text on a slide.
  • 4c. Home is a complete list of both Ideas and Fixes and the amount of progress done in each.
